# Agent Brief: Revamp drivebelt.pro to Make TurnVest the Main Focus

## Objective
Redesign the Drivebelt website so TurnVest is the clear primary product and conversion target. Consulting should remain present, but demoted to a secondary motion. The current site leads with broad Salesforce consulting language. The revised site must feel like a focused SaaS product website built to drive AppExchange installs, demo requests, and credibility.

## Current Problems to Fix
1. The hero currently leads with general consulting messaging instead of TurnVest.
2. The page structure centers "Our Expertise" service categories rather than a product story.
3. Navigation/footer placeholders appear unresolved and make the site feel unfinished.
4. There is no strong product-led CTA path for AppExchange, demo booking, or product exploration.
5. The site lacks a clear visual product narrative: problem, dashboard proof, outcomes, screenshots, FAQ, and trust signals.
6. Consulting should support the product story, not compete with it.

## Primary Conversion Goal
Push visitors toward one of these actions:
- View TurnVest on AppExchange
- Book a demo
- Contact Drivebelt about TurnVest

## Secondary Goal
Present Drivebelt consulting services as a follow-on offering for implementation, optimization, data mapping, onboarding, and broader Salesforce FinOps/architecture help.

## Positioning
TurnVest is a Salesforce cost optimization and usage analytics product that helps companies see where Salesforce spend is going, identify waste, and make smarter renewal decisions across multiple orgs.

## New Site Structure
Rebuild the homepage around this sequence:

### 1. Hero Section
Replace the current hero with a product-led section.
The primary goal is for website vistors to click the link to the listing on the appExchange https://appexchange.salesforce.com/appxListingDetail?listingId=67fda616-7603-4aea-a06e-56bc9bd65c9f
Keep the minimal theme and some of the color scheme you can take liberties if theyre tasteful.

#### Hero copy direction
Headline options:
- Take Control of Your Salesforce Spend
- See Exactly Where Your Salesforce Spend Is Going
- Reduce Salesforce License Costs and Eliminate Waste

Subheadline direction:
TurnVest gives you real-time visibility into Salesforce license spend, unused cost, and cost per user across every org—so you can act before renewal.

Primary CTA:
- View on AppExchange

Secondary CTA:
- Book a Demo

Hero visual:
Use the best TurnVest dashboard visual or animated product still. Show money metrics prominently, especially total spend and unused spend.

### 2. Problem / Pain Section
Add a short section that makes the pain obvious.

Use 3-4 tiles or short rows:
- Multiple Salesforce orgs
- No unified spend visibility
- Unused licenses hidden in plain sight
- Renewal decisions made without hard data

This section should feel sharp and commercial, not fluffy.

### 3. Product Proof Section
Add a product section with real screenshots from TurnVest.

Use the screenshot concepts already prepared:
- Connect Every Salesforce Org—Instantly
- See Total Spend—and Exactly What You're Wasting
- Instantly Identify License Waste & Optimization Opportunities
- Know Your True Cost Per User Across Every Org
- Drill Into Spend by Org. No More Blind Spots

Each screenshot block should have:
- headline
- one sentence of value
- clear crop/highlight

### 4. How It Works
Add a 3-step or 4-step flow:
1. Connect Salesforce orgs
2. Aggregate usage and license cost data
3. Surface waste, cost per user, and optimization opportunities
4. Make smarter decisions before renewal

This should visually mirror the hub-and-spoke concept.

### 5. Outcomes / Benefits Section
Make outcomes highly specific and tied to buyer value.

Examples:
- Unified cost visibility across all orgs
- Identify unused and underutilized licenses
- Understand cost per user and unit economics
- Reduce waste before contract renewal
- Support FinOps-style Salesforce governance

### 6. Trust / Credibility Section
Replace generic testimonial filler with stronger credibility.

Possible content:
- Built specifically for Salesforce cost visibility
- Designed for multi-org enterprises
- Salesforce-native product experience
- Consulting expertise available for rollout and optimization

If real customer quotes are not available, do not use fake-sounding testimonials. Use proof points, architecture credibility, or implementation expertise instead.

### 7. Consulting Section (Secondary)
Keep consulting, but move it lower on the page and reframe it.

Suggested headline:
Need Help Beyond the Product?

Suggested copy direction:
Drivebelt also provides Salesforce FinOps, DevOps, integration architecture, and implementation support for teams that want help rolling out TurnVest or improving broader Salesforce operations.

This section should not look equal in weight to the product hero.

### 8. Final CTA Section
End with a strong product CTA.

Suggested headline:
Ready to See What Your Salesforce Spend Is Really Costing You?

Buttons:
- View TurnVest on AppExchange
- Book a Demo

## Navigation Changes
Update the top navigation so it is product-led.

Suggested nav:
- Product
- Screenshots
- How It Works
- Consulting
- Contact

Also fix any unresolved placeholder nav/footer tokens.

## Design Direction
- Keep the site clean, modern, and product-centric
- Prefer a premium SaaS feel over a generic consulting-site feel
- Use screenshots, product stills, and subtle motion
- Reduce stock-service-card styling
- Stronger visual hierarchy in the hero
- Make CTA buttons obvious and repeated throughout
- Use whitespace generously
- Use one consistent visual system tied to TurnVest

## Messaging Rules
- Lead with product outcomes, not consulting breadth
- Talk about spend, waste, visibility, cost per user, and renewal readiness
- Avoid vague enterprise buzzwords
- Keep copy concise and commercial
- Product first, consulting second

## Content to Remove or Demote
- The current "Our Expertise" section as the main homepage content
- Generic consulting-first hero copy
- Weak/generic testimonials if not real
- Any filler content that does not support product conversion

## Technical / UX Requests
- Ensure AppExchange CTA opens the correct listing
- Add a demo/contact CTA path
- Optimize homepage for desktop first, then responsive mobile
- Keep performance reasonable even if hero includes animation
- If animation is used, provide static fallback image

## Deliverables
1. Rewrite homepage information architecture around TurnVest
2. Replace hero copy and CTAs
3. Add product screenshot/story sections
4. Move consulting to a secondary section
5. Fix nav/footer placeholder issues
6. Improve overall visual polish to feel like a focused SaaS product page

## Implementation Priority
Priority 1: Hero, CTA, nav, product positioning
Priority 2: Product screenshots and how-it-works flow
Priority 3: Consulting repositioning and trust section
Priority 4: Final polish, responsive tuning, and animation treatment

## Success Criteria
The homepage should make it obvious within 5 seconds that:
- TurnVest is the main product
- It helps reduce Salesforce waste and improve spend visibility
- Visitors can immediately go to AppExchange or request a demo
- Drivebelt consulting exists, but as support around the product
